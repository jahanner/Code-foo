The total floor area in square feet of the Empire State Building minus the ground floor is 2,684,701. Which would mean I need 343,641,728 Lincoln Logs for the total floor area assuming the floors are one foot thick. It's 1252 ft. high and 187 ft. wide according to thiswebsite giving each side an approximate area of 233,750 sq ft.
